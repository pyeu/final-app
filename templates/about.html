About Deaths in Custody

Data

My data comes from the Deaths in Custody Reporting Program , a program within the Bureau of Justice that collects data on deaths that occur during the arrest process or while inmates are in the custody of local jails or state prisons. I found the data through MuckRock thanks to Dan's explainer on how to filter MuckRock requests by Status == completed.
